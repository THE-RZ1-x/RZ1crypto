Mining vs. Staking

Mining (Proof of Work)

  • Requires powerful hardware
  • High energy consumption
  • Competitive rewards
  • Higher initial investment

Staking (Proof of Stake)

  • No special hardware needed
  • Energy efficient
  • Predictable rewards
  • Lower barrier to entry
